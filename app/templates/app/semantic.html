What we do offer In Specific Search

Search By Party

This search criterion allows you to search for cases by the names of the parties involved. Parties refer to the individuals or entities who are involved in a legal dispute.

Search By Judgement Date

This search allows you to search for cases based on the date of the judgment. This can be helpful in researching recent cases or cases from a specific period.

Search By Bench

In legal systems with multiple judges or panels, the bench refers to the group of judges who hear and decide a case. Find cases associated with a particular bench or panel of judges.

Search By Court

Different jurisdictions have multiple courts, such as trial courts, appellate courts, and supreme courts. Find cases heard in a specific court or level of the judicial hierarchy.

Search By Judge

Searching by judge enables you to find cases associated with a particular judge. This criterion helps when you want to study the decisions rendered by a specific judge.

Search By Topic

This search criterion allows you to search for cases based on specific legal topics or subjects. Find cases related to a particular area of law or legal issue.

Search By Act & Section

Legal cases often involve the interpretation and application of specific statutes or sections of legislation. Find cases that refer or discuss provisions of a statute.
