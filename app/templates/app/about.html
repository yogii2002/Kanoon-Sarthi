About Us

Who we are

{% endcomment %} {% comment %}

Kanoon Sarthi is an AI-powered legal search system designed to revolutionize legal research and case analysis. Built with advanced natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning techniques, the system enables users to find specific legal cases, discover advanced case combinations based on multiple legal features, and predict potential legal outcomes. Kanoon Sarthi goes beyond traditional keyword-based search by offering intelligent recommendations of similar cases, contextual legal insights, and predictive analysis, making it an invaluable companion for lawyers, judges, law students, and researchers. By automating and enhancing the legal search process, Kanoon Sarthi aims to improve the speed, accuracy, and efficiency of legal decision-making.

{% endcomment %}

The Background Story

The Digital India Mission launched by the Government of India in 2015 served as a beneficiary for many other schemes or initiatives and transformed the country as a whole. The legal industry is one of the major drivers for the stability of the society and the economy and thus it must be digitized. The major challenges observed in this process are unstructured and vague representation of the documents, legal jargon, inadequate signposting of key information, multiple interpretations of the information, only a subset of the entire Acts and Laws is relevant in context, and non-adaptability of the stakeholders for the digital shift.

Objectives

Our primary objectives at Kanoon Sarathi are:

Enhancing Legal Accessibility:

We strive to improve access to legal information for both legal professionals and the general public. Through our multilingual platform, we aim to break down language barriers and provide easy-to-understand legal resources.

Empowering Stakeholders:

We aim to empower judges, lawyers, litigants, and legal researchers by offering advanced tools and functionalities. Our platform enables efficient document representation, analysis, and search capabilities, enabling stakeholders to make informed decisions.

Promoting Legal Literacy:

We believe in promoting legal literacy among the public. By providing open access to Acts, Laws, Court Cases information, and other legal resources, we aim to enhance understanding and awareness of the judicial system.

Facilitating Precedent Discovery:

Kanoon Sarathi is designed to assist legal professionals in finding similar cases and precedents based on contextual analysis. This functionality helps in strategic case preparation and supports the development of robust legal arguments.

Assisting Judicial Decision-Making:

Through AI-powered algorithms and machine learning, we provide insights and predictions to assist judges in their decision-making process. Our goal is to help judges assess the acceptance ratio of appeals and petitions with explainability.

Streamlining Legal Processes:

We aim to streamline legal processes by leveraging technology and automation. Our platform offers features such as virtual court integration, online consultations, and document management tools to enhance efficiency and reduce administrative burdens.

Features And Benefits

Kanoon Sarathi offers a range of advanced features and benefits that enhance the accessibility, efficiency, and decision-making capabilities within the Indian Judicial System. Our platform provides the following key features:

Multilingual Interface:

Kanoon Sarathi is designed with a multilingual interface, allowing users to access legal information and resources in their preferred language. This feature promotes inclusivity and ensures that language is not a barrier in accessing legal knowledge.

Document Structuring:

Our platform employs advanced technologies to structure legal documents, making them more organized and easy to navigate. This feature simplifies the understanding and analysis of complex legal texts.

Simplified Language and Signposting:

Kanoon Sarathi utilizes simplified language and effective signposting techniques to improve the readability and comprehension of legal information. This feature makes legal content more accessible to a broader audience.

Powerful Search Functionality:

Our AI-powered search functionality enables users to quickly and accurately search for specific legal information, Acts, Laws, Court Cases, and relevant documents. Users can perform specific searches to narrow down their results based on keywords, categories, or other filters. This feature saves time and effort in retrieving the desired information.

Advanced Search Capabilities:

Kanoon Sarathi provides advanced search capabilities, allowing users to refine their search queries with more complex criteria. Users can utilize Boolean operators, proximity searches, wildcard searches, and other advanced search techniques to retrieve precise and targeted results.

Find Similar Cases:

Our platform employs contextual analysis and machine learning algorithms to find similar cases based on the content and context present in the court case documents. This feature assists legal professionals in identifying relevant case precedents and supporting their legal arguments. It saves time and effort by automatically discovering similar cases that may have similar legal issues or outcomes.

Virtual Court Integration:

Kanoon Sarathi seamlessly integrates with virtual court systems, allowing users to participate in virtual hearings, file cases online, and engage in online consultations. This feature facilitates remote access and communication, especially in times of crisis or geographical constraints.

Advanced Document Analysis:

Our platform utilizes natural language processing and machine learning techniques to analyze legal documents. This feature enables users to extract key insights, identify relevant information, and discover patterns within large volumes of legal data.

Legal Judgment Prediction:

Kanoon Sarathi employs AI algorithms to assist judges in predicting the acceptance ratio of appeals and petitions. This feature provides valuable insights and reasoning to support judges in their decision-making process.
